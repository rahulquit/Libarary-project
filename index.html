About Our Library

Baba Ruba Das Library is a place where dreams take shape. We provide students with the right resources, peaceful environment, and guidance to help them build their careers. Whether you’re preparing for government jobs, competitive exams, or personal knowledge growth, we are here to support your journey.

  • Well-stocked books for competitive exams
  • Peaceful and comfortable reading spaces
  • Motivational seminars and workshops
  • Guidance for career and personal growth
